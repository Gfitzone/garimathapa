<h1 align="center"><font color="orange">Business communication</font></h1>
<p>
<h3>Business communication refers to the process of transferring business data and information from one place to another through communication media.
</h3>
</p>

<h2>Features of business communication.</h2>
<ol type="1"><font size="4">
<li>communication facilitates the exchange of information</li>
<li>communication is a continuous process</li>
<li>The purpose of communication is to bring mutual understanding among the receiver and the sender</li>
<li>communication takes place between atleast two or more than two parties</li>
</font>
</ol>

<h2>Modes of communication</h2>

<ol type="1"><font size="4">These are the contemporary bussiness communication modes
<li>Written</li>
<li>Oral</li>
<li>Visual</li>
<li>Digital</li>
</font>
</ol>

<h2>Functions of Business communication</h2>
<ol type="1"><font size="4">Internal functions
<li>The main function of business communication is transmitting and sharing information</li>
<li>Communication related to incentives,compensation,job performance etc helps to motivate the employees</li>
<li>Internal communication helps develop emotional connection among members of the organization</li>
</ol>
<ol type="1"><font size="4">External functions
<li>promotional communication such as advertisement,social medial contents etc helps create brand awareness  </li>
<li>External communication helps build strong relationship with the customers.Thus,building the company's image</li>
</ol>
